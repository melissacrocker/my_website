COVID-19 Statistics Viewer

As COVID-19 cases continue to rise at alarming rates across the United States, Colorado is no exception.

This visualization is an attempt to investigate how different counties are impacted within Colorado.

You can use the dropdown to examine the data by different variables that may lead to interesting insights about how COVD-19 cases are related to poverty and insurance status.

This data is compiled from several sources. The COVID-19 case numbers come from USA Facts and are current as of October 31, 2020. The insurance and poverty data are from the United States Census and are estimated for 2018.

The insurance data is can be found at SAHIE and the poverty data can be found at SAIPE.
